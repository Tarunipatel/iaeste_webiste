IAESTE Day Edition

The International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) is an independent, non-profit, and non-political student exchange organization. With more than 90+ member countries and over 4000 traineeships exchanged each year, it is the largest organization of its kind in the world. LC Manipal University Jaipur, established in the year 2013, aims to help its members realize their dreams and facilitate the exchange of ideas, both technical and cultural, by connecting students from various cultures and countries. Throughout the years, IAESTE India LC MUJ has hosted a diverse variety of interns from across the globe and has succeeded in sending its skilled members as interns to other nations.

On October 20, IAESTE fraternity celebrates 'The IAESTE Day.' What is IAESTE Day, you ask? Well, it's a day where we celebrate our wonderful association and all the people in it, from our members to our volunteers, from our trainees to our alumni, and from our employers to our academics. We intend to promote international understanding and celebrate the IAESTE spirit of 'Work! Experience! Discover!'

Let's celebrate the IAESTE essence, all the wonderful people, and the opportunities it provides. So, wherever you are, raise a glass and celebrate with us.

IAESTE India LC MUJ wishes you a very 'Happy IAESTE Day!'

IAESTE Day Celebrations

The COVID-19 pandemic came out of the blue and brought life to a virtual standstill. We are fortunate enough to have been blessed with such innovative technologies that can bring two people 1000s of miles away together in just a fraction of seconds.

At IAESTE, we firmly believe that when the roots are deep, there is no reason to fear the wind, and Alumni is a strong force that binds us with our roots of wisdom and expertise. It's been a long time, but it's never late to share our experiences, memories, and wisdom with each other.

To celebrate the spirit of IAESTE, all the wonderful people and experiences, we called upon our alumni for a virtual get-together, and to say the least, it was nostalgic. It was all about sharing experiences, the fun that our alumni had during their internships, and the friends they made along the way. The meet focused on how IAESTE had brought out the best in our alumni and how IAESTE is truly "Work! Experience! Discover!"

Achintya Mohan, former President, IAESTE India LC MUJ (2015-16), shares his experience, "The time in the LC will teach you skills that will be invaluable for you in your career ahead. And an internship abroad will not only broaden your horizons in your technical field but also help you in becoming a truly global citizen. IAESTE will leave you with a bucket full of memories, which will make you nostalgic for all the years to come. So if you are willing to carry this albatross around your neck for the rest of your life, aboard the ship, close your eyes, and let the IAESTE magic begin!"

It was a walk down the memory lane to relive the IAESTE days and celebrate the IAESTE essence.

Pandemic Response

What will the world look like after the COVID-19 pandemic? How will it shape the work and markets? - these are the questions popping up more and more, as slowly economies are opening up from lockdown, and people are trying to position themselves in this new reality.

Remote working has been a growing trend in the workforce in recent years. However, until now, it has not been widely adopted. The COVID-19 outbreak and subsequent social isolation measures have caused companies to explore remote working options and implement collaborative tools in an attempt to sustain their business through the crisis.

The home office isn't a just benefit anymore. Being able to work remotely is a new reality.

The current scenario creates challenges and hardships for everyone, but the world is much more open to remote work and overcome the difficulties and losses due to the pandemic. To overcome the challenges, IAESTE came up with an alternative approach- IAESTE Remote Internships.

IAESTE Remote Internships gives you a chance to form international friendships and gain valuable work experience. At the heart of IAESTE Remote Internships lies quality. Each internship is created and defined in such a way as to ensure giving students valuable work experience. As always, we care about each trainee and provide social and intercultural experience online and at home.

Reet Nandy, an Outgoing Intern, shares his Remote Internship Experience, "Connecting me with people from across the world even in the middle of a pandemic, is what IAESTE did for me. I was not only given the opportunity to work under experienced faculties but even let me expose myself to a different culture and new experiences. IAESTE Experience is something that cannot be explained but needs to be felt."

IAESTE Remote Internships are an additional way, alongside IAESTE's world-known internship exchange program, to connect even more students and employers and build bridges between cultures by using the latest technologies.

So, tune in, and get ready to experience the best. Join IAESTE today!
